We're Open!

Welcome to Vahego Coffee! We are a small, family-owned coffee shop located on Westminster Street, in Providence, Rhode Island. We are less than 5 minutes (walking distance) away from PPAC. We even have our own parking lot. Our customers shouldn't be subjected to using parking meters.

What do we sell?

If it's related to coffee, we almost definitely sell it. Coffee grinders, ground coffee, brewed coffee, pastries and desserts, and more. We sell both industrial-grade and home-sized equipment, you can buy coffee in almost any weight (online must be at least .5lbs due to shipping & handling fees). Unfortunately though, there is one pastry we do not sell... We do not sell donuts with which to dunk in your coffee.
