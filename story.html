New Website!

On May 2nd, 2017, the first official release of the new and improved Vahego Coffee website is released! Following our recent in-store remodel, we here are pleased to announce the release of our new and improved website.

Well, what changed?

For starters, we are now supported on mobile and tablet devices! Previously, we only supported computer-sized screens and users reported numerous issues with using our website on mobile. As of now, you can use our website on any size screen, and you will be able to experience a smooth, unique, and exciting website! Browse our selection of coffee, equipment, and many other products with ease! Look through news about our store and products, and learn more about us as a company and as coffee lovers as whole. As always, our mission is to bring you the perfect cup of coffee. We sincerly hope you enjoy the new and improved website, and we would like to give an especially big thanks to all of our customers who have stuck with us through this in-store and online renovation and remodeling period! Thank you all, and enjoy the brand new and improved Vahego Coffee!
